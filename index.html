Ogre Battle

-- Adventure's Handbook --

Use these tips to fight effectively and not get killed!

  1. Health: Keep track of your health and the Ogre's. Remember, if your health reaches 0, you lose!
  2. Damage: How much health is deducted when a hit is landed.
  3. Attack: Swing your weapon to damage the Ogre.
  4. Defend: Reduce 100% of the damage of the next attack.
  5. Power Attack: Will deal more damage than a normal attack. Beware of the Ogre's Power Attack!
